FindUrSpace - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is FindUrSpace?

FindUrSpace helps you find the perfect coworking space across India, making the search easy for freelancers, startups, and businesses in lesser time without any cost.

2. How does FindUrSpace work?

  1. Submit your workspace preferences.
  2. Get AI-based space recommendations.
  3. Schedule visits via AI bot over a call.
  4. Post-visit, confirm with managers and finalize your booking.

3. What types of workspaces can I find?

You can find day passes, meeting rooms, hot desks, private offices, manager cabins, and fully furnished managed spaces tailored to your needs.

4. Which cities does FindUrSpace cover?

FindUrSpace lists coworking spaces in cities like Bangalore, Mumbai, Delhi, Gurgaon, Pune, Hyderabad, Lucknow, Indore, and more.

5. What makes FindUrSpace different?

We offer AI recommendations, zero brokerage fees, and a quick appointment scheduling at the space, ensuring a hassle-free booking process.

6. Is FindUrSpace free to use?

Yes, FindUrSpace has zero brokerage fees for finding and booking coworking spaces.

7. Can I book a coworking space right away?

Yes, after receiving AI recommendations, you can quickly schedule and book an appointment at the space.

8. What benefits do coworking spaces offer?

Coworking spaces offer flexible options, amenities like high-speed internet, and networking opportunities.

9. How can I contact FindUrSpace?

You can use the AI-human bot on the website.
