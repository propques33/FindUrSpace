FindUrSpace - Cancellation & Refund Policy

At FindUrSpace, we aim to offer a seamless booking experience. Below are our cancellation and refund terms:

1. Day Passes

  • Cancellations 24+ hours before start time: Full refund.
  • Cancellations under 24 hours: Non-refundable.

2. Meeting & Conference Rooms

  • 48+ hours before: Full refund.
  • 24–48 hours before: 50% refund.
  • Less than 24 hours: Non-refundable.

3. Monthly Memberships

  • 30-day written notice required for cancellation.
  • No refund on early termination.

4. Refund Process

  • Processed within 21 working days to original payment method.
  • Service/transaction fees will be deducted.

5. Non-Refundable Scenarios

  • Cancellation after start time.
  • No-shows.
  • External issues (network, power, etc.) at user’s end.

6. Contact for Refunds

Email: buzz@propques.com

FindUrSpace reserves the right to update this policy at any time. Please review it periodically.
