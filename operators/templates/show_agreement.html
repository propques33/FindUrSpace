Agreement Between FindurSpace.Tech and {{ agreement.coworking_name }}

This Agreement is made and entered into on the date of acceptance between FindurSpace.Tech (a trademark of PropQues Services Pvt. Ltd.) ("Platform") and the undersigned space operator ("Operator") collectively referred to as the “Parties”.

1. Listing Terms and Conditions

1.1 The Operator agrees to list their coworking space on the Platform and abide by the Platform's terms and conditions which may be updated periodically.

1.2 The Operator agrees to provide accurate, complete, and updated information regarding the coworking space being listed on the Platform.

2. Commission on Successful Transactions

2.1 The Platform will charge the Operator a commission of 10% of the deal value on each successful transaction.

2.2 Deal Value will be calculated as follows:

  • If the agreement between the Operator and the client includes a lock-in period, the Deal Value is monthly rental × number of months in the lock-in period.
  • If there is no lock-in period, the Deal Value will be calculated based on the tenure of the agreement (monthly rental × number of months).

2.3 Renewal Commission: In the event the Operator and the client renew their agreement, the Platform will charge a commission of 10% of the deal value for the renewal period calculated in the same manner as the initial commission.

3. Migration to Yearly Platform Fees

3.1 The Platform may in the future migrate to a yearly platform fee model instead of the current commission structure. If such changes are implemented, the Operator will be notified in advance.

4. Featured Space Listing

4.1 If the Operator wishes to list their coworking space as a Featured Space, the Operator agrees to pay a monthly fee as determined by the Platform.

4.2 Featured Spaces will be prominently showcased on the Platform in accordance with the terms of the Featured Listing section.

5. General Terms

5.1 The Operator acknowledges that by listing a space on the Platform, they accept all terms including any modifications that may occur in the future subject to notification.

5.2 This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the Parties regarding the subject matter hereof and supersedes any prior agreements or understandings.
